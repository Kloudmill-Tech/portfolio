## Overview of Cookies in Browsers

:memo: A **Cookie** is a text file embedded in web browers that serve a specific purpose. Automatically saved to our browsers, after navigating a website, cookiestrack user activity. Think of this text file as marker that notes our behavior and report back to the website owner. Additionally, there used by marketing companies to deliever ads tailored to your interest. Searching for snowboards, will deliver ads on planning your next ski trip to Aspen, Colorado. That is the magic or albeit technical marvel of cookies.
